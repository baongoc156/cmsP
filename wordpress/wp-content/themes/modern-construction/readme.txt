== Description ==

Modern Construction WordPress theme is bold, powerful and stunning, designed specially to cater all the real estate businesses and services such as construction and building, contractor work, property dealing, real estate agency and broker-ship, repair, architecture, structure, fabrication, manufacture, renovation, real estate, invoices ,accountants, agents ,consultants road builders, carpenters, brokers, township, raw material building material, builders, interior designers, useful for blog, corporate or portfolios trading and any such relevant business. From serving the basic to advanced needs of builders and contractors, Modern Construction WordPress theme is capable enough to design a performance efficient website over a click. The code is optimized for faster page loading. It is readily responsive, custom logo, Microformats, cross-browser compatible and multilingual. This theme allows creating a portfolio, showcasing projects and services and publishing the most recent news and blog in the most professional manner. This multipurpose, mobile-first theme is SEO friendly and integrated with social media icons. As the theme is multipurpose, it works perfectly for other business websites apart from architects and construction profession.
